About Cozy Corner

The Cozy corner Cafe was founded by Muzhgan Elyasi in 2022.
It is located in Pole-sorkh in Kabul Afghanistan. This Cafe is founded
to be a place where all afghan women can come to enjoy their time
with their lovely friends with having a cup of coffee, sweet talks,
and even can use from the books which are available in the Cafe.

Things Cozy Corner gives to you

  1. Enjoyment
  2. Good memories
  3. Happiness
